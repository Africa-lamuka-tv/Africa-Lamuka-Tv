Inyandiko shingiro ya Africa Lamuka TV

Afurika ifite ubutunzi bwinshi, ariko Abanyafurika ni abatindi;
Afurika ifite abaturage bafite ingufu, ariko ni bo bahora bicwa mu ntambara za mpatsibihugu;
Afurika ifite abaturage basenga cyane, ariko ni bo bahorana ibyago n’amakuba.

Twebwe abashinze uru rubuga Africa Lamuka TV tumaze imyaka irenga 10 dukora ubushakashatsi ku mpamvu zitera Afurika kugira ingorane zavuzwe haruguru. Dusanga ikibazo nyamukuru Abanyafurika bafite ari ikibazo cy’IMITEKEREREZE n’IMYUMVIRE.

Ubushakashatsi twakoze bwerekanye ko imibereho ya muntu (mibi cyangwa myiza) ituruka mu buryo yitegereza isi n’ibiyiriho, uburyo abisesengura n’imyumvire abikuramo, kuko ari byo bigena ibikorwa akora ngo atunganye imibereho ye.

Iyo witegereje ibikorwa umunyafurika akora, ari na byo akesha amaramuko, usanga ari ibi bikurikira:

Kwiga (Ishuri) : Abanyafurika bizera ko nta yindi nzira yo kwigobotora ubukene itari ishuri.

Idini : Abanyafurika barasenga cyane, bakizera ubuzima bwiza nyuma yo kuva muri iyi si yuzuye ibyago.

Ubuyobozi na politiki : Abanyafurika bumvira ubuyobozi, bakabuyoboka kuko bibwira ko bubabereyeho kubereka inzira nziza y’imibereho.

Icyo Abanyafurika batamenya ni uko ishuri, idini n’ubuyobozi ari ibikoresho twahawe n’Abakoloni, kandi ko iyi si dutuye ari isi y’inyungu aho nta rukundo rubaho iyo igihe kigeze cyo kuburwanira.

Ibi bikoresho bitatu bikoreshejwe mu kuyobya imitekerereze n’imyumvire y’umunyafurika, kugira ngo adakangukira gushakisha ubutunzi no kwirukana mpatsibihugu.

Ishuri ribereyeho gushyira umwana mu gihome (icyumba cy’ishuri) kuva ku myaka 7 kugeza kuri 25, igihe imitekerereze ye iba iri kubakwa. Muri icyo gihe, tumwigisha byinshi ariko tukamuhisha aho ubutunzi nyakuri buherereye: zahabu, diyama, coltan.

Idini rikorwa mu buryo butuma abantu badakangukira kwigobotora mpatsibihugu, ribigisha kwihanganira imibabaro, aho kwirwanaho.

Ubuyobozi na politiki bikoreshejwe mu gushyiraho amategeko abuza umuturage kwegera ubutunzi, mu gihe mpatsibihugu zikomeza kuyobora zica inyuma.

Izi nzego eshatu ni ibikoresho by’ubukoloni. Igihe kirageze cyo kubisuzuma no kubiha isura nyakuri bifite.

Ni yo mpamvu hashyizweho Africa Lamuka TV nk’urubuga rukangurira umuturage kumenya impamvu y’imibereho mibi ye n’inzira zo kuyivamo.

2. Imbaraga eshatu zigena imibereho ya muntu

Imibereho y’umuntu ituruka mu mbaraga eshatu z’ingenzi:

1. Imbaraga z’Umuremyi (Force Créatrice)

Ni imbaraga zigena ibyaremwe byose, zigendera ku mahame 6: ubumwe, ihuriro, gahunda, uburinganire, ubwenge n’ubushobozi bwo kwiyuburura.

2. Imbaraga z’Ibiriho (Force matérielle)

Zibumbiye mu ndemabintu zihora zihinduka, zikabyara ibishya, ibishaje bigashira.

3. Imbaraga z’Umutima (Force spirituelle)

Ni imbaraga zitekerezwamo n’amarangamutima bigena imyitwarire y’umuntu.

Iyo imbaraga z’umutima zidakoreshejwe neza, izindi mbaraga zirahindukira zikabangamira umuntu aho kumufasha.

  • Umuntu utagira ubumenyi ntashobora kwirwanaho
